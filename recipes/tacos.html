Tacos

Description

These mouth watering tacos are the last food you'll ever have to make. They are the ultimate combination of flavors and are sure to make you want more!

Not only are they incredibly simple and cheap to make, they're enough for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey
  • Lettuce
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Fully cook the ground turkey and leave over a low heat
  2. Warm up your tortillas on a skillet over a low heat
  3. Add turkey and cheese to the tortilla
  4. Garnish with lettuce and salsa
