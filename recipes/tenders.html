Tenders

Description

These homemade chicken tenders are my own recipe. Through trial and error I perfected the art of making some good ol tendies.

You'll be amazed how easy it is to get great flavor and healthy tenders in such a short amount of time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut your chicken breast to the desired size
  2. Egg wash the chicken and roll it in the breadcrumbs
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Air fry at 400 for 12 minutes
  5. Enjoy with your favorite sauces
